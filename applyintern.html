Success in Service is a TED-style program featuring diverse, passionate Returned Peace Corps Volunteers who share their stories about how service impacted their personal and professional trajectory. As part of this internship, you will organize a Success in Service event on your campus.

Benefits

  • Flexible schedule that fits around your classes
  • Free reign to be creative with your Success in Service event
  • One-on-one mentorship from dedicated staff members in the Peace Corps Office of Innovation in Washington, DC
  • Network with accomplished Returned Peace Corps Volunteer speakers working in a variety of fields

Event Coordinating Intern

Responsibilities

  • Determine event space, date, and time
  • Find and invite potential speakers
  • Confirm speakers and prepare for talks
  • Set up and organize event space
  • Manage volunteer team and administer tasks

Qualifications

  • Ability to manage a team
  • Can quickly come up with creative solutions
  • Effective community organizer
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Self-motivated and self-starter
  • Work under minimal supervision
